Added ``daytime_checkout/lsstcam_checkout.py`` to automate LSSTCam image acquisition checkout and MTOODS ingestion.
It performs the following steps:
 * it checks that MTCamera CSCs are enabled.
 * it takes a ``bias`` exposure and verifies it is ingested by MTOODS.
 * it takes a ``engtest`` exposure and verifies it is ingested by MTOODS.
